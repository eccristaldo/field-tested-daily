The Backup Power Dilemma

Most homeowners think they only have two realistic options for backup power:

Option 1: Complete Solar System - Professional solar panel installation with battery backup typically costs $25,000-$40,000. While effective, this investment is out of reach for many families, and ROI can take 15-20 years.

Option 2: Gas Generators - Portable or standby generators range from $500-$15,000, but require regular maintenance, fuel storage, produce noise and fumes, and may not work during extended outages when fuel becomes scarce.
